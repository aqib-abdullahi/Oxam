Features

Easy to Use

User-friendly and straight foward interface for both students and administrators. For students, this means straightforward access to their enrolled courses and available exams, while instructors can efficiently manage courses and exams.

Secure

Robust security measures to protect exam data and user information. Role-based access control ensures that users, including students and instructors, only have access to the specific features and data that are relevant to their roles.

Exam Availability Control

Students can only access exams that have reached their start times, preventing premature access. This feature adds a layer of time-based access control, ensuring a fair and organized examination process.

About

This is a project inspired by the unending spiral of the manual marking and grading of students in an institutional setting. The errors committed by either examiners and others that partake in processing of results has been cut. Oxam web application is designed with a primary focus on user-friendliness. We understand that both students and administrators need an intuitive and straightforward interface to streamline their tasks and enhance their experience. This is also a Portfolio Project for Holberton School.
